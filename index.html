About Me

Personal Introduction

Hello, my name is Joonsung Byun, and I am pursuing a degree in web design and development at Utah Valley University. I have a strong passion and interest in the combination of web technology and creativity. My desire to create modern and effective web applications is what motivates me to have educational and career goals.

Present Educational Goal

At present, I am studying web design and development at Utah Valley University. This program delves deeply into fundamental web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, I am enhancing my design skills using tools like Adobe Photoshop and Figma. My academic achievements include successfully completing various web development projects and aiming to improve my technical competence in web design and frontend development. I am particularly interested in user-centered design and optimizing the performance of web applications.

An Entry-level Career Goal Statement

My current educational goal is to enter the field of frontend development. I am passionate about improving user experiences and aspire to create clean and modern web applications. I plan to take advanced courses in the UVU web design and development program, and I am committed to mastering major frontend frameworks like React and Svelte. Given the ever-growing importance of web technology and design, I believe frontend development offers a dynamic and promising career path.

A Long-range Career Statement

In the long term, my goal is to build an in-depth understanding of big data and data processing, which are essential components of the Fourth Industrial Revolution. I intend to merge this knowledge with frontend development, creating innovative and contemporary projects. By learning languages like SQL, I aim to effectively combine data processing and frontend development, contributing to the development of data-driven web applications. Big data and data processing will play a pivotal role in the future of web development and data technology, and I am committed to further advancing my expertise in these areas.
